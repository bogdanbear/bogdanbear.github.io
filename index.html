Bogdan Ursu

Hi! I am a researcher in cryptography at Consensys, where I work on efficient arguments of knowledge. In Spring 2023, I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from ETH Zurich, under the supervision of Prof. Dennis Hofheinz. During my PhD, my main research interests were at the intersection between zero-knowledge, functional encryption and indistinguishability obfuscation.
