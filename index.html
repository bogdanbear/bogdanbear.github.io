Bogdan Ursu's Homepage

Hi! I am a PhD student at ETH Zurich, under the supervision of Prof. Dennis Hofheinz. My main research interests lie at the intersection between indistinguishability obfuscation, functional encryption and zero-knowledge.

  • I have graduated from the Parisian Master of Research in Computer Science as a student of École Normale Supérieure de Cachan.

  • Before, I have obtained my Bachelor of Computer Science from University Alexandru Ioan Cuza of Iasi.
