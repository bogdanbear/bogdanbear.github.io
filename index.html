Bogdan Ursu (Bear)'s Homepage

Hi! I am a PhD student at ETH Zurich, under the supervision of Prof. Dennis Hofheinz. My main research interests lie at the intersection between indistinguishability obfuscation, functional encryption and zero-knowledge.

  • Parisian Master of Research in Computer Science - École Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay.

  • Bachelor of Computer Science - University Alexandru Ioan Cuza of Iasi.
