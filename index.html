About me

| I am a second year master student at École Normale Supérieure de Cachan, where my main directions of study are cryptography, complexity and computability. I have graduated my first year of master with a thesis on shortest vector problem solvers, carried out under the supervision of Prof. Serge Vaudenay, at the EPFL. Before, I have obtained my Bachelor of Computer Science from University Alexandru Ioan Cuza of Iasi, where I have worked with Prof. Ferucio Laurentiu Tiplea. As an undergraduate Erasmus student, I have also followed the research master in cryptography at Université Joseph-Fourier, most notably the courses on elliptic and hyperelliptic curve cryptography taught by Ms. Vanessa Vitse. |
